Inspiration

My paper covers the idea of remastered games. When I was younger, I was given a DS for my 6th birthday. I loved playing Mario Bros., where I would pass the DS around with my Dad. I continued to play these titles, and I loved playing video games to which I started coding on my computer. Although I would love to code video games. I turned to coding little programs for fun. Now, I am CS major. Since I am older now, games that I used to play are being remastered, which now looking back at these titles, I feel nostalgic for them. Thus, that is why I focused on nostalgia and video games because I enjoyed them when I was younger

Research Findings

After researching, I understood that a person may want to play remastered titles to reconnect with their past self. Their past self thus played these older titles when they were first released.

Quote 1

“we’re less interested in the gaming experience than we are in recreating the memories associated with them”

The Counterargument

I noticed that many people commented that these remastered games are poisoning the gaming industry as it is a repeat of reusing older gameplay and mechanics. However, if the main reason for some is to enjoy their experience and reconnect with their past self, I believe it is ok if there is a little repetition.

Quote 2

“we’ve reached the point where gamers will even fund creators to indulge their nostalgia for them”
